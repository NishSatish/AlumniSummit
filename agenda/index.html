annual homecoming agenda

CENTRAL SESSION

A short opening ceremony to greet all the Alumni present whilst summarising 75 years of the institution. An event that will focus on the future of BMSCE and how the Alumni Network can be incorporated to help the institute grow. The event will be followed by scholarship announcements for the students.

10:00AM to 11:00AM
11:00AM to 1:00PM
open house

An open house that showcases the history of all departments such as the batch photos, events, placement statistics, and many more. The departments will display their finest projects and research papers followed by a short talk by the HOD on the future of the department and how the alumni can contribute to the same.

Career Fair - Higher Education

A specialization-specific interactive session between the students and the alumni who pursued their higher education to provide the students with an overview of what is required and what needs to be prioritized to get admitted to the best colleges.

2:00PM to 3:00PM
3:00PM to 4:00PM
Career Fair - Jobs & Internships

An interactive session between the students and the Alumni who are hiring for specific roles in their organization. This is a great opportunity for students to explore their interests and options following the needs of job providers.

Entrepreneurship Meet

Alumni turned entrepreneurs and investors network with students in this session to establish peer-to-peer connections & learnings while students get a platform to pitch their ideas and seek mentorship, business connections, investments, and much more!

2:30PM to 3:30PM
2:00PM onwards
Fun events

An event to refresh and rejoice! A fun quiz, followed by a standup comedy session and a musical evening. A perfect way to end the Alumni Summit.
