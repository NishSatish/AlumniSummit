About our Annual Alumni Day - Homecoming 2022

The Annual Alumni Day is an annual hosted by the BMSCE Alumni Network. This time around, to celebrate 75 years of BMSCE, the Alumni Summit is going to be bigger and better! Not only does this event bring together BMSCE alumni from across the world, it also brings new opportunities for the students to connect and learn.

An event that will witness most, if not all, of BMSCE Alumni participating to network and reconnect with everyone in the institution. From fun informal events to entrepreneurial events, the Alumni Summit has it all covered. A great platform for both the students and the alumni to interact with each other and help each other grow.
