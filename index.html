About our Annual Alumni Day - Homecoming 2022

The Alumni Day is an annual event hosted by the BMSCE Alumni Network. This time we will be celebrating our 75 years of BMSCE, Alumni Day which is going to be bigger and better!
This event not only brings together BMSCE alumni from across the world, it also brings new opportunities for the students to connect and learn. An event that will witness most of the BMSCE Alumni participating to network and reconnect with everyone in the institution.
We are hosting informal events to entrepreneurial events, the Alumni Day – Home coming 2022 has all of it covered. A great platform for both the students and the alumni to interact with each other and help each other grow. Trust us when we say this is an opportunity you wouldn’t want to miss!
